Services

My experience across engineering, infrastructure, and tech-driven environments allows me to offer support global teams by transforming complex information into accessible, multilingual formats. I specialize in structured writing, expert translation, and clear visual communication - ensuring every piece of content is both technically accurate and easy to navigate.

The services I provide include:

  • Technical Writing & Documentation
    • User manuals, help files, GUI/CLI guides, release notes, application notes, infographics, and training content for engineering platforms such as EDA tools, MES systems, and hydropower applications.
  • Documentation Strategy & Workflow Optimization
    • Auditing and restructuring documentation workflows; implementing doc-as-code practices and platform migrations; optimizing and aligning documentation tools with Agile and DevOps workflows.
  • Multilingual Translation & Localization
    • English ⇄ Russian ⇄ Ukrainian translation for a range of technical disciplines including hydroengineering, electromechanics, architecture, civil works, telecommunications, geology, and environmental consulting. Also available for bid documents, tenders, and marketing materials.
  • Terminology Systems & Glossary Development
    • Building bilingual glossaries, aligning cross-disciplinary language, integrating CAT tools, and supporting terminology systems for structured, scalable use.
  • Training & Onboarding Materials
    • Creating product training decks, internal guides, UX onboarding flows, tooltips, style guides, and materials for remote and multilingual teams.
  • Visual Documentation & Infographics
    • Crafting concept diagrams, wireframes, process flows, and visual content that supports user understanding and product positioning.
