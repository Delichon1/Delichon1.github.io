A few words about me

Interdisciplinary by education with longterm practice — I work at the intersection of technology and communication. With a background in applied mathematics and philology, I like clarifying complex systems through structured documentation.

As a technical communicator, I specialize in multi-language documentation for IT and engineering platforms (MES, EDA, CAD), blending visual design with agile workflows and tool-driven processes. My work spans user guides, system manuals, and onboarding content — with experience in docs-as-code, XML, and terminology management. I thrive both in-office and remotely, independently or within collaborative teams.

All my professional life, I've supported international projects for software and engineering firms — contributing not only as a writer, but as a bridge between development, design, and localization.
